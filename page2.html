Trevi Fountain in Rome, Italy is such an amazing and beautiful place.

Tossing Coins into Trevi Fountain

  • Throwing a coin into the fountain with your right hand over your left shoulder will ensure your return to Rome.
  • Throwing two coins will help you find romance
  • Throwing three coins to marry an Italian in Rome
